# ABConvert Interview Talking Points

## Core Positioning

I am not presenting myself as a traditional manager who only coordinates teams, and I am not presenting myself as someone who simply knows how to prompt AI tools. My value is that I can use AI as part of a disciplined engineering workflow to shape requirements, build systems, debug issues, and keep shipping quality under control.

## What I Want Them To Remember

1. I am AI-native but still hands-on.
2. I am strong at turning ambiguity into a working system.
3. I care about workflow guardrails, verification, and release quality, not just speed.

## Proof Point 3: UltraTrading

- Built a multi-runner crypto trading and paper-trading system with closed-loop learning, deploy verification, and audit layers.
- Solved engineering reliability problems such as schema drift, silent failures, reconciliation, and unsafe RPC usage.

## Why I Fit An AI-Native Startup

- I can work in a fast-moving environment where requirements are still forming.
- I am comfortable combining product judgment, system design, and direct implementation.
- I already think in terms of context engineering, verification gates, handoff quality, and iterative refinement.

What I bring is not just AI coding speed. I bring a way to make AI-assisted development more reliable, more scalable, and more useful for real product delivery.
